About Us

Welcome to the Emotion Detection App! Our mission is to leverage the power of artificial intelligence to analyze emotions from facial expressions, helping users understand and explore their feelings in innovative ways.

This project combines cutting-edge AI technologies with a user-friendly interface to provide real-time feedback and insights. Whether for fun, education, or research, this app makes emotional analysis accessible to everyone.
