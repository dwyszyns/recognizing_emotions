About Us

Welcome to the Emotion Detection App – designed to support individuals with autism in learning and recognizing basic facial expressions and emotions. Our goal is to harness the power of artificial intelligence to provide an engaging and educational tool that helps users better understand social cues and emotional expressions.

This project integrates advanced AI technologies with a simple, user-friendly interface, offering real-time feedback and insights. Whether for learning, personal development, or support in social interactions, this app aims to make emotional recognition more accessible and intuitive.
